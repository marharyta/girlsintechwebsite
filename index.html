About The Event

Girls in Tech Helsinki goes FROSMO: The Coding Day is a practical workshop on the topics of web coding & tools on October 29, 2016.

This 1-day Workshop offers attendees the chance to finesse existing skills while mingling with peers, forming new relationships and networking with professionals from the region.

GIT Helsinki goes Frosmo: The Coding Day is open for anyone interested in developing and improving their coding skills. This 6 hours workshop has been designed for upper beginners with already some basic knowledge in HTML/CSS/JavaScript languages and will be conducted with the assistance of coding coaches from Frosmo.

The event is free of charge and open for both women and men.

The event has capacity for 20 people and the application period deadline is October 9,2016 at 23:59. All the applicants will be notified of the results on October 17, 2016 via e-mail. Please bring your own computer for this session.

Hosted By

FROSMO is the forerunner in the Single Tag based UI improvement methodology.
Thanks to the Frosmo platform, online business owners can achieve unprecedented freedom, speed and cost-efficiency in developing unique experiences for their online customers. Our customers are large online businesses who want to create winning User Experiences online, and who don’t want to be limited by a small set of predefined features.
Living up to the Best Exponential Startup title, Frosmo is growing fast and actively seeking talented people to join our international team. Frosmo’s Single Tag UI Development method opens up incredible opportunities to have an impact on the future of the internet. Check out our open vacancies on http://frosmo.com/en/careers

Girls in Tech (GIT) is a global non-profit focused on the engagement, education and empowerment of girls and women who are passionate about technology. Girls in Tech Helsinki started its operations in May 2015 and is the first GIT chapter in the Nordics.

Before applying

These tests help verifying that your starting level of HTML&CSS (JavaScript - only basic things) is enough to enjoy our workshop to the fullest. To submit your tasks, please create an account on codepen.io and create code snippets ('pens') with your code. Then save them and attach links to your application in the appropriate field.
